About Me

I’m a strategic B2B product and marketing leader with 17 years of experience driving digital transformation and telecom innovation across Iraq, Pakistan, and the wider MENA region. I specialize in developing, launching, and monetizing advanced ICT and connectivity solutions, including DIA, MPLS, IPLC, SIP Trunking, cloud, and managed services.

Throughout my career, I’ve led the full lifecycle of over 20 impactful products, from concept and go-to-market strategy to portfolio expansion and revenue acceleration. My work has helped organizations like Asiacell, Zong (China Mobile), and PTCL establish new revenue streams, optimize infrastructure, and lead in competitive markets. I pioneered Iraq’s first IPLC and NNI POP with China Mobile and built the country’s first full-spectrum voice product line (FL Voice).

I’m passionate about ecosystem building, collaborating with global vendors, and empowering cross-functional teams. Whether it’s enabling cloud transformation, executing digital-first GTM campaigns, or aligning commercial goals with customer needs, I bring a techno-commercial mindset that delivers results.
